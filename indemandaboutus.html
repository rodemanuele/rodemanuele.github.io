ABOUT US

InDEMANd Automotive was established in August of 2024. We are owned and operated by Red-seal Automotive Service Technicians with over 10 years of experience in the automotive industry. Our technicians provide regular care and maintenance and are familiar with all makes, models, and types of vehicles. We can do everything from minor and major repairs to wild customizations. In addition to the care and passion put into all work performed on your vehicles, InDEMANd Automotive offers many levels of interior and exterior detailing at reasonable prices. InDEMANd Automotive specializes in custom exhaust packages, suspension lifting and lowering packages, as well as engine swaps and performance enhancement.
